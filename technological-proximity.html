Technological Proximity

Technological Proximity and Follow-On Invention

Tejas Ramdas studies technological proximity as a condition shaping how firms use generative inventions and how exposed they are when inventions implicate existing knowledge elements, product attributes, and strategic commitments.

This work connects technological proximity, innovation search, search incursion, generative inventions, patents, and technology strategy.
