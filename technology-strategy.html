Technology Strategy

Technology Strategy and Innovation

Tejas Ramdas studies technology strategy: how enabling technologies and generative inventions change firm search, appropriability, competitive positioning, and downstream value creation and capture.

This research uses patents, text-as-data, network methods, machine learning, and econometrics to understand how firms compete around emerging technologies before product-market rivalry becomes visible.
