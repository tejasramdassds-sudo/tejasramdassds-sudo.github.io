Research Profile

Strategy research with a computational spine.

My work examines how firms search, learn, and compete around emerging technologies. I combine strategy theory with large-scale patent data, text-as-data methods, machine learning, causal inference, and econometric design.

Research

Core Themes

Innovation Search

How firms move through technological space, recombine knowledge, and direct inventive activity beyond their established knowledge bases.

Technology Strategy

How enabling and generative technologies change competitive positioning, appropriability, and downstream opportunities for value creation and capture.

Networks and AI

How organizational and technological networks shape learning, coordination, invention, and strategic response in data-rich environments.
