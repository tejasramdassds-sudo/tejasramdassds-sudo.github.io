Sequential Entry

Sequential Entry and Appropriation

Tejas Ramdas studies sequential entry and appropriation in enabling technologies with Gautam Ahuja. This work examines how firm entry over time shapes technology strategy, competition, and value capture in enabling technology settings.

The project connects sequential entry, appropriation, enabling technologies, innovation strategy, and empirical work on patents and firms.
