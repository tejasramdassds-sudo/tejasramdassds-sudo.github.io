Generative Inventions

Generative Inventions and Firm Search

Tejas Ramdas studies generative inventions as inventions that open broad downstream possibilities for follow-on inventive activity. His work with Gautam Ahuja examines how generative inventions can enable firms to move beyond their own prior inventive activity and toward knowledge domains associated with other firms' prior strengths.

This research connects generative inventions, innovation search, search incursions, technological proximity, patents, technology strategy, and appropriability.
