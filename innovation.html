Innovation Research by Tejas Ramdas

Tejas Ramdas studies innovation as a strategic and organizational process. His work examines how firms search through technological space, how inventions become generative, and how firms compete around the knowledge foundations of future products and capabilities.

At Cornell University, this research combines strategy, technology and innovation, organization theory, patents, machine learning, networks, computational social science, and econometrics.
