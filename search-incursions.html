Search Incursions in Innovation Strategy

Tejas Ramdas uses the concept of search incursion to describe inventive search directed beyond a firm's own prior inventive activity and toward knowledge elements that other firms had previously developed more strongly.

The construct is part of a broader research program on innovation search, generative inventions, technological proximity, patents, and competitive movement in technological space.
