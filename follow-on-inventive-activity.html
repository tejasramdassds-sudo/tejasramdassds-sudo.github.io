Follow-On Inventive Activity

Follow-On Inventive Activity and Generative Inventions

Tejas Ramdas studies follow-on inventive activity by tracing how firms build on generative inventions and how those follow-on inventions may move into knowledge regions associated with other firms' prior strengths.

The research uses patents, text embeddings, innovation search measures, and firm-level strategy theory to study technological competition before product-market entry is visible.
